  • Description

    We are looking for an Electrical Instrumentation, Control and Automation Engineer to carry out control systems, integration and electrical design duties for a unique programme for our varying industrial clients.  In this role, you will be responsible for delivering detailed controls and electrical design work within a multidisciplinary engineering team. A good practical understanding of operational plant and control systems is essential. As well as experience in PLCs, I/O modules, industrial communication protocols, industrial networks, and IoT, which will include management and programming - this will include the ability to write bespoke logic per project. The ideal candidate will have the ability to read and fault find from circuit diagrams, perform control systems, network diagnosis, and develop control software.  Previous experience of on-site working is a must. You will be reporting to our Head of Engineering.
