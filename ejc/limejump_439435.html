  • Description

    As our Engineering Manager, you will lead two development teams: the trading desk and trading platform team. Together, the mission of these teams is to provide tools that enable traders to make more effective trading decisions. You will work with multiple teams, especially the platform team who build APIs to expose pertinent data and ensure that a consent stream of trade data flows through the platform ready for asset dispatch. The desk team compliment this, by working directly with traders to build tooling to interact with the platform. You will in this position report directly into the CTO.

  • Required
    • Previous line management experience.
    • A passion about culture and performance.
    • Solid understanding of end-to-end product delivery.
    • Experience with cloud native technologies.
    • Be an advocate for best practice and thorough testing.
    • Be an effective communicator who is comfortable explaining technical content to nontechnical audiences.
    • Several years experience as a software developer writing code for production web services, preferably leveraging Python/PostgreSQL/Django/Celery.
  • Responsibilities

    • Assist in making platform architecture decisions.

    • Participate in strategic initiatives with our parent company Shell.
    • Ensure that we are choosing appropriate technologies and building software in accordance with best practices.
    • Line manage software developers by providing mentoring and personal development guidance through regular 1:1s.
