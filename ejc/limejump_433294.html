  • Description

    You will as our Technical Lead drive the architecture and implementation of our asset scheduling system; a core piece of our platform that receives various types of trades and balancing instructions as input and converts those into asset schedules for energy delivery. The latency demands on schedule changes can range from hours right down to second level responses presenting quite the engineering challenge! While this role requires strong architectural abilities, you will also be writing code and providing support and mentorship to our less experienced developers daily. In addition to day-to-day work on the asset scheduling system as a Technical Lead at Limejump you will also play a role in defining platform wide architecture, identifying technical debt and coordinating cross team initiatives. You will report into our Engineering Manager.

  • Required
    • Python, Django and Go.
    • Postgres
    • Kafka / event-driven systems.
  • Responsibilities
    • Committed to quality and good testing practises.
    • Able to think holistically about system design.
    • Proven experience in mentoring more junior developers.
    • Able to communicate effectively with stakeholders & developers.
  • Desired
    • Passion and commitment towards team leadership.
    • Experience with low-latency delivery systems.
    • Kubernetes / container-based deployments.
    • Passion for sustainability energy and/or experience within the industry.
