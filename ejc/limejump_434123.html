    Limejump seeks an outstanding Product Manager to help us change the world of energy. If you have a track record of delivering complex projects in the Electricity Industry and using data to inform your decisions, then please read on to learn more about our opportunity. As our Product Manager, you will drive strategy and planning for our Asset Scheduling Engine, including Flexibility market integration, dispatch profile creation and optimisation. This role is entrepreneurial in its nature and requires a UK electricity market focus that's complemented by sound understanding of technology and IoT systems. You will be collaborating closely with the Technology and Commercial aspects of Limejump to develop innovative products that champion our company mission and will report to Limejump's Head of Product. What You'll Do:

  • Required
    • Experience with UK Electricity Markets and IoT Platforms.
    • A passion for Limejump's mission - to revolutionise the energy industry.
    • Ability to analyse and use data to make informed product decisions.
    • Exceptional communication skills, both verbal and written.
    • Outstanding attention to detail and drive to continuously improve internal resources.
    • Ability to thrive in a dynamic and fast-paced work environment.
    • A drive to use data, research, and well-honed judgment to ensure your work delivers the best possible products.
    • Outstanding analytical and technical skills, with an ability to challenge and be challenged on your numbers.
  • Responsibilities
    • Lead our product process from discovery, definition and all the way to release/learn phase.
    • Write robust user stories and acceptance criteria to drive forward our commercial offering.
    • Manage complex dependencies while building resilience in your team.
    • Effectively manage the business priorities and drive Limejump's Data Science roadmap.
    • Build relationships and work closely with stakeholders to effectively capture Limejump's product requirements and opportunities.
    • Own and champion product plans, objectives, progress against plans and KPIs to all stakeholders.
    • Integrate research and market analysis into optimisation tool requirements to enhance product quality.
    • Drive team development and awareness while continuing to professionally develop as a subject matter expert.
  • Desired
    • UK Flexibility Market Knowledge
    • Understanding of APIs and utilising AI
    • Experience with Battery and/or Gas Operations (dispatch scheduling)
    • Product Manager experience within the Sustainable Energy Industry.
