  • Description

    We want you as our new Senior Data Engineer to be apart and help Limejump shape a more sustainable world of cleaner energy. You will be responsible for contributing to and helping lead the architecture, design and roll-out of a new enterprise-wide data platform. Your primary responsibility is to oversee the development of our new data warehouse, built on top of Snowflake. You will oversee architecture and design, as well as hands on building out of infrastructure and pipelines; likely leveraging Confluent Cloud/Kafka. You will also take on a wider role supporting data strategy too, whilst being supported from people across the business including data scientists, platform engineers and a BI analyst.
