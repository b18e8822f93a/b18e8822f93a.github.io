  • Description

    We want you as our new Senior Data Scientist to be apart and help Limejump shape a more sustainable world of cleaner energy. You will be contributing to and helping lead data science and machine learning projects, with a focus on using modern methods for improving the performance of a variety of forecasting models. Your primary responsibility is to further improve Limejump’s data science and machine learning capabilities, solutions and production services, with a particular focus on time series analysis and forecasting. You will spend time both improving the performance of existing forecasting models, as well as developing greenfield forecasts in new areas for the business. As a senior member of the team, you will also mentor the more junior members of the team, and operate as a subject matter expert across the business.

  • Required
    • Good verbal and written communication skills.
    • Proven experience working with time-series data, ideally in real-time.
    • Be a master problem solver and can understand and tackle complex problems.
    • Expertise with machine learning and deep learning techniques and frameworks, especially those used for time series prediction.
    • Demonstrated ability to work with large volumes of data, such as for forecasting and real-time control of assets, preferably processing live data streams.
    • A flexible, innovative mindset: this is a fast-moving often complex environment where you will have to take responsibility for solving difficult problems.
    • A degree (BSc/ MSc/ PhD) in a quantitative subject (Maths, Physical Sciences, Computer Science etc…).
    • 4+ years of experience in using Python for commercial data science work within data-intensive environments - ideally in the energy, tech, finance, or consumer goods sector.
  • Responsibilities
    • Designing, building and deploying data science solutions, such as: -Creating and improving machine learning models for forecasting. -Creating market-leading algorithms that are used to operate and/or manage distributed energy assets, including in real-time. -Build and validate models, applications, and services for guiding decisions by analytics, commercial, trading and operations teams.
    • Contribute towards Limejump's data science strategy and roadmap.
    • Maintain strong relationships with internal and external stakeholders.
    • Responsible for various projects and team management.
    • Explore how machine learning methods, processes, and techniques can be further applied to core Limejump challenges, and present key findings internally and externally.
  • Desired
    • Experience in Bayesian statistics and/or econometrics.
    • Top Data Science credentials (such as Kaggle Masters).
    • Experience in developing or supporting forecasts within the energy sector.
    • Further degree in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Data Science (MSc/ PhD)
    • Ideally, we are looking Full Stack Data Scientist; as such you would be expected to understand the business and industry context, conceptualise solutions, extract and prepare data, develop models, write production-ready code, deploy the models and algorithms, and track their performance
