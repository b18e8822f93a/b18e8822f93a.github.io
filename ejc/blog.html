First Post

Welcome to this new job search website, focussed solely on the energy-trading sector.

As developer working on the front office, I keep an eye on the jobs market and have noticed over the years that there isn't a single repository to explore and easily get an overview of the current opportunities available, either being advertised directly through the hiring companies or recruiters.

I have prioritised simplicity, no cookies, no need to login or submit any personal data, just a simple search bar to filter vacancy, some detail on each job and a link to the application page.

I hope it assists in your job search, good luck.
